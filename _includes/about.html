About the Erie Outing Club


The Erie Outing Club is a non-competitive social outdoors club for adults (and teens accompanied with a guardian) We schedule events throughout the year and enjoy hiking, backpacking, kayaking, bicycle riding, and in the wintertime snowshoeing, and cross-country skiing in the Erie region and beyond.


Whether you're a seasoned outdoor enthusiast or just starting out, we welcome you to join us on our activities. The Erie Outing Club is "self-propelled" -our members initiate events. We're a friendly and supportive group, and we love sharing our love of the outdoors with others. So come join us for outdoor adventures.
