About the Erie Outing Club

COVID-19 updates- During the Pandemic, we endeavor to follow CDC and State guidelines to stop the spread of covid-19 (wearing a mask, and physical distancing 6ft or more frequent Handwashing, indoors and outdoors. If you feel sick, was in contact with a covid postive person, Please stay home)

We are a social, non-competitive Outdoors Club for adults, who participate in all kinds of outdoor activities. We schedule outdoor activities throughout the year, generally in the Erie PA area and surrounding counties, including western NY and northeast Ohio. We mostly like to go camping, hiking, backpacking, cycling, and kayaking; in the wintertime, we cross-country ski and snowshoe but are open to doing other activities also. We enjoy socializing and will sometimes get something to eat after an event in a safe way. The groups.io site is the communication system for members of the club.
