About the Erie Outing Club


The Erie Outing Club is a non-competitive social outdoors club for adults (and teens accompanied with a guardian).



We schedule events throughout the year and enjoy hiking, backpacking, kayaking, bicycle riding, and in the wintertime snowshoeing, and cross-country skiing in the Erie region and beyond.



Whether you're a seasoned outdoor enthusiast or just starting out, we welcome you to join us on our activities. Members initiate events, which they lead. These activities are posted on our website calendar.



We're a friendly and supportive group, and we enjoy sharing our love of the outdoors with others. So come join us for outdoor adventures.
